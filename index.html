About Me

As a devoted stay-at-home mother of four, engaged in the noble task of home educating my children, I embarked on a transformative journey towards becoming a digital marketer. Recognizing the need for personal and professional growth, I took the proactive step of enrolling in a Level 3 digital marketing course, all while navigating the intricate tapestry of homeschooling alongside my children. The decision to delve into the realm of digital marketing was driven by a sincere aspiration for a future career change. This journey has proven to be nothing short of inspiring, as it not only aligns with my lifelong passion for computing but also broadens the horizons of my capabilities. Throughout this educational expedition, I have eagerly absorbed the latest trends and advancements in the dynamic field of digital marketing. From social media strategies to search engine optimization, each module has contributed to a comprehensive understanding of the industry's ever-evolving landscape. Looking ahead, I am enthusiastic about the prospect of applying this newfound knowledge to carve out my path as a freelancer digital marketer. The synthesis of my familial responsibilities and academic pursuits has equipped me with a unique perspective and a tenacity to navigate the challenges that lie ahead. I eagerly anticipate the opportunity to blend my passion for digital marketing with my commitment to family, creating a fulfilling and dynamic career that aligns seamlessly with the intricacies of my life.
